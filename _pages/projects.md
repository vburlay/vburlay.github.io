# Projects

A selection of technical and analytical projects developed over the last years,
covering computer vision, forecasting, predictive analytics,
API development, and operational analytics.

The projects combine machine learning,
time-series analysis, analytical system design,
and decision-oriented data workflows.

---

## Computer Vision

### [Severstal](https://github.com/vburlay/Severstal)

Detection and localization of surface defects on steel plates
using deep learning methods.

The project combines:

- image segmentation using **U-Net**
- binary defect classification with **ResNet50**
- defect localization workflows
- industrial quality-control use cases

The goal was to improve automation and efficiency
in steel production quality inspection.

---

## Forecasting & Time Series

### [Rossmann Store Sales Forecasting](https://github.com/vburlay/Rossmann)

Time-series forecasting and analytical exploration
of retail sales dynamics based on the Rossmann Kaggle dataset.

The project focuses on:

- exploratory time-series analysis
- trend and seasonality decomposition
- forecasting pipelines
- lag and rolling-window feature engineering
- promotion and calendar effects
- operational sales dynamics

The work combines statistical reasoning,
feature engineering, and forecasting-oriented analytical workflows
using Python, SQL, and DuckDB.

## Predictive Analytics

### [Allstate Claim Prediction Challenge](https://github.com/vburlay/allstate_claim_prediction)

Predictive modeling of automobile liability insurance claim payments
based on vehicle and policy characteristics.

The project includes:

- exploratory data analysis
- regression modeling
- insurance risk prediction
- interactive visualization with **R Shiny**

## API Engineering

## Decision Intelligence

### [IQ Early Warning System](https://github.com/vburlay/risk-forecast-intelligence)

Operational decision intelligence prototype designed
to detect emerging risks, forecast instability,
and support proactive decision-making under uncertainty.

The system combines:

- operational monitoring
- forecasting and baseline modeling
- anomaly detection
- probabilistic risk assessment
- scenario simulation
- intervention analysis

Core focus areas include:

- temporal risk dynamics
- early warning signals
- operational steering
- team-level risk prioritization
- time-to-risk estimation
- decision-support logic

The prototype is built on real operational insurance data
and explores how analytical systems can transform
raw operational signals into structured decision intelligence.
